About TechAtFarm

We are an organisation which helps the agricultural communities (NGOs/Firms) in providing farmers with modern facilities like Automated Irrigation (through Wi-Fi sensors), Farmer Search portal for listing their crops for SALE, Crop Search recommending farmers about seasons, pesticides, fertilizers etc., BlogSpot featuring articles on new techniques by our experts. It creates suitable database for farmers which helps them with adding their lands and make requests for irrigation facility etc. on their Dashboard.

We are expert in







Automated Irrigation

We provide automated systems to farms connected by Wi-Fi/other means to our databases and sensors which perform regular moisture tests and irrigate the land as per requirements of your Crops.

Farmer Search

A facility to Food Processing Industries and businesses which need to acquire crops at suitable prices from farmers registered in our Database. Just search by crop name and get contacts of farmers along with their quoted prices and acquire crops directly from them without involving the middlemen.

BlogSpot

BlogSpot is a wonder destination for farmers to look into every day to get insights about how to improve their farming practices and what new farming technologies are in trend these days.

Crop Search

Crop search is a facility for registered users to get recommendations by our experts on moisture requirements of different crops, suitable temperature range, pesticides, fertilizers etc. just by typing in the crop name
